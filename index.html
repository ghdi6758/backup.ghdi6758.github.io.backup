April 7-11th, WWW, Seoul, Korea

Jisun An is a Post Doctoral Researcher in Social Computing team at Qatar Computing Research Institute (QCRI). Her research interests are in social media, news, bias, and politics. Her research lies at the intersection of machine learning, network science, social science, and human computer interaction.

She received her PhD in Computer Science from a Computer Laboratory at the University of Cambridge/ For her study, she was funded by EPSRC and she is now an honorable recipient of Google European Scholarship. Since starting her PhD, she has been fortunate to have opportunities to collaborate with pioneers in social network analysis (e.g., MPI-SWS (Germany), KAIST (South Korea), PARC (USA), and Yahoo! Barcelona (Spain)). By working with them, she has enriched her knowledge and ability to tackle and solve fundamental issues in the field. Before joining Cambridge, Jisun completed BS and MS in Computer Science and Engineering with a concentration in Social Computing at Korea Advanced Institutes of Science and Technology (KAIST) in 2007 and 2009, respectively.
